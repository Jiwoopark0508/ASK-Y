Askers

"Ask-Y" is an Interactive, Collaborative, Question-based video learning platform.
You can make higher learning acheivements by asking questions. However, many of you have experiences with feeling shy when asking question. It is natural since asking question requires higher level thinking processes. This system gives you a question template that you can reference to help you generate questions easier. So, let's learn by ask!
In this platform, you are expected to do followings.
Watching Lecture
Asking 2~3 Questions
Discuss with Others
Let's ask!
